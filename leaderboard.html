# Move Player Teams GP Points

GP = total matches played by this player's teams. Click a name for full stats. Arrows update after each score sync.

Scoring System

Group Stage

  • Win — 3 pts
  • Draw — 1 pt
  • Loss — 0 pts
  • 1st in Group — +3 pts bonus
  • 2nd in Group — +1 pt bonus

Knockout Stage

  • Round of 32 — +2 pts
  • Round of 16 — +4 pts
  • Quarter Finals — +6 pts
  • Semi Finals — +9 pts
  • Runner Up — +12 pts
  • Winner — +15 pts
